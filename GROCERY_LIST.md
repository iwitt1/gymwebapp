# Grocery List — This Week (pared for 1 person)

Scaled for **one person, limited fridge space**. The plan repeats a few meals instead of buying 18 different things, and leans hard on the **4 lb of chicken thighs you already have** (that's most of your protein — no need to buy much meat). Cook in **2–3 day batches**, not the whole week at once.

**Already have — skip:** chicken thighs (4 lb), chia, quinoa, pasta, tomato-basil sauce, Calabrian chile, capers, artichoke hearts, honey, spices, olive oil, balsamic, soy sauce, teriyaki.

## 📅 This Week's Meals (6 days, meals repeat)

Repeating on purpose so nothing spoils. Cook a batch, eat it 2–3x.

| | Breakfast | Lunch | Dinner |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 1 (today)** | Eggs + toast + spinach | **Calabrian chile chicken pasta** (quick) | Sheet-pan chicken thighs + potatoes + veg *(cook extra)* |
| **Day 2** | Smoothie | Leftover sheet-pan thighs | Turkey tacos *(cook the full 1 lb → 3 servings)* |
| **Day 3** | Eggs + toast | Turkey burrito bowl *(leftover turkey + quinoa + salsa + guac)* | Calabrian chicken pasta again |
| **Day 4** | Egg + soy chorizo hash | Chicken-thigh quinoa bowl + peppers + salsa *(cook extra)* | **TJ's variety night** — carne asada *or* chile lime burgers + salad |
| **Day 5** | Smoothie | Leftover thigh bowl | Turkey tacos again |
| **Day 6** | Eggs + toast | Whatever's left / thigh + quinoa | Easy button — Mandarin Orange Chicken + veg *(or leftovers)* |

**Snacks (optional):** hard-boiled eggs · banana + sunflower butter · smoothie.
**Day 7:** free / eat-out / clear-the-fridge day.

### The whole week runs on 4 cook sessions
1. **Sheet-pan thighs** (Day 1) → dinner + Day 2 lunch
2. **1 lb ground turkey** (Day 2) → tacos ×2 + a burrito bowl
3. **Calabrian chicken pasta** (Day 1 & 3) → fresh, ~12 min each
4. **Quinoa + thigh bowls** (Day 4) → dinner-adjacent + Day 5 lunch

Everything else is eggs, smoothies, and leftovers — minimal fridge footprint.
